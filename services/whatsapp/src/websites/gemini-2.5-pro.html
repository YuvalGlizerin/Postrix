Our Story

Our cozy flower workshop

Founded from a small studio with a big heart, Bloom & Petal was born out of a lifelong love for botany and design. We believe that flowers are more than just decorations; they are a vital part of life's most cherished moments.

We source our flowers from local, sustainable growers to ensure the freshest, most vibrant blooms for your arrangements. Our team of artisan florists pours their creativity and expertise into every single bouquet, ensuring a unique and personal touch.
